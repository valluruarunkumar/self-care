Self Care !


Self-care is the practice of protecting and improving your physical and mental health. The scope of self-care is broad, but the key word in its definition is practice. So, it includes anything you do on a regular basis, with or without the direct support of a healthcare professional, to keep a healthy, balanced lifestyle.

  • Maintaining good personal hygiene
  • Eating a nutritious diet
  • Getting enough hours of sleep, and ensuring that your sleep is actually restful and restorative
  • Engaging your body in a mix of exercise and relaxing activities
  • Speak to yourself kindly.
